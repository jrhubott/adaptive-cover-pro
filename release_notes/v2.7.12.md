## 🎯 Fix: Horizontal Cover Closes Before End Sun Time

### 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Fixed horizontal covers closing prematurely while sun is still in FOV** — When the vertical calculation saturates at full window height (common at large gamma angles), the awning gap becomes 0, yielding a 0% position. This caused open/close-only covers to receive `close_cover` while the sun was still within the field of view. Position is now clamped to at least 1% when the sun is actively hitting the window, ensuring covers stay open during sun tracking.
