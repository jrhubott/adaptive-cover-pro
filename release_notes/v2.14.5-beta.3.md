## 🛑 Integration Kill Switch & Safety Controls

### ✨ Features

- **Integration Enabled switch** — a true master kill switch for ACP. When OFF, every outbound cover command is blocked: solar, climate, custom position, manual-override expiry, startup, end-of-window default, Force Override, and Weather Override. Motion and weather deferred timers are cancelled. Reconciliation state is cleared so no stale target replays on re-enable. Diagnostic sensors continue updating. Flip it back ON and covers stay in place; the next natural update cycle resumes normally.
- **Immediate stop on disable** — when Integration Enabled flips OFF, ACP immediately sends `cover.stop_cover` to any cover currently in motion (capability-checked).
- **Global services** — three new services for multi-instance / whole-home control:
  - `adaptive_cover_pro.emergency_stop` — stops every configured cover on all ACP instances, then disables.
  - `adaptive_cover_pro.integration_disable` — stops in-flight moves and disables.
  - `adaptive_cover_pro.integration_enable` — re-enables; covers stay in place.
  - All three accept a `target:` block (entity, device, or area) to narrow scope.
