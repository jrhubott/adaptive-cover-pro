## 🎯 Sync Categories & Duplicate Flow

### ✨ Features

- **Sync category selection** — When syncing settings to other covers, you can now choose which setting groups to copy (Window Dimensions, Sun Tracking, Climate, Automation, etc.) instead of syncing everything at once. The confirmation screen clearly lists which categories and which covers will be updated.
- **Duplicate cover flow** — When adding a new integration instance, a menu now appears offering "Create new cover" or "Duplicate from existing cover". The duplicate flow lets you pick a source cover and copies all its settings to a new window with a different azimuth and entities.
