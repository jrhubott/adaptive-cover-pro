## 🐛 Bug Fix: Reset Button Race Condition

### 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Fix manual override re-detection after reset button press** — After pressing the reset button to clear a manual override, the cover's settling movement was incorrectly being detected as a new manual override. The reset button now suppresses override detection during the post-reset cover movement and lifts suppression immediately after the cover settles.
- **Add 30-second timeout to reset button wait loop** — The wait-for-target loop now has a 30-second timeout to prevent the button press from hanging indefinitely when a cover never reports the exact target position (e.g. due to position rounding or a stuck cover).
