## 🐛 Fix: Physical device remains visible after clearing association

### Bug Fix

- **Physical device no longer lingers under integration after unassociating** — When a device association was cleared (reverted to "None (standalone device)"), the previously-linked physical device remained listed under the integration entry with no entities. On reload, the integration now explicitly removes its config entry link from any physical device that no longer has integration entities registered to it.

### Notes

This is a follow-up fix to `v2.7.10-beta.3`. If you see a "ghost" physical device under your integration entry after clearing the association, update to beta.4 and reload the integration to clean it up.
