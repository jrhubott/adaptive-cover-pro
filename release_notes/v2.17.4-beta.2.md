## 🐛 Fix: Cloud suppression and climate positions clamped by sun-only limits (#105)

### 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Cloud suppression now reaches default position when sun-only max limit is configured:** When a "during sun tracking only" max position limit was set (e.g. 26%), cloud suppression was incorrectly clamping the default position (e.g. 50%) to that limit. Covers stayed at the max limit instead of moving to the default when clouds were detected.
- **Climate mode (winter heating, summer cooling) no longer clamped by sun-only limits:** The same issue affected climate handler positions — winter heating (100%) and summer cooling (0%) could be clamped by a sun-only max limit when the sun was geometrically in the field of view.
